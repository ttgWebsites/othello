Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Othello is a very famous play written by William Shakespeare telling of the tragic downfall of a veteran general from Venice. An aging Othello falls in love with a young woman, Desdemona, and chooses to secretly marry her. The marriage appears to be wonderful at first however when Othello's job as a general takes him to Cyprus, a small island, his marriage and life will never be the same. From the beginning two men, Iago and Roderigo, plot to upset the relationship between Desdemona and Othello, and the setting of Cyprus provides the perfect opportunity for this. The close proximity between the characters allows Iago, the antagonist, to execute his tragic plot and convince Othello of his wife's untrue infidelity. The accusations cause Othello to snap and react in a violent manner murdering his wife therefore bringing about a tragic downfall to a very noble and well-respected man.
